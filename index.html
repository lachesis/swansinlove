Places to Visit

A selection of our favorite places in Cincinnati.
  • Cincinnati Museum Center
    A former railway station, the CMC boasts famous exhibits from around the world and the children's museum where Patricia completed her undergraduate research.
  • Cincinnati Zoo
    Always a fun place to visit, take the chance to say hello to their wide variety of exotic animals including Fiona the baby hippo.
  • Newport Aquarium
    Surround yourself with all the fish in the sea as you stroll through the terrifying Newport Aquarium tunnel.
  • Cincinnati Art Museum
    One of Patricia's favorite places to visit and runner-up for our wedding venue. Take a stroll around their rotating art galleries or their permenant collection of Cincinnati related works.
  • Over the Rhine
    Once the poorest part of Cincinnati, OTR is a new symbol of Cincinnati's revival. Check out historic buildings and delcious food and drink. The Eagle is particularly tasty.
  • National Underground Railroad Freedom Center
    As one of the southern-most Northern cities, Cincinnati played a pivotal role in the Underground Railroad. Visit the Freedom Center to learn more.
